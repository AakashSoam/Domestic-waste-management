Recycling Guidelines

Plastics

Dispose of plastic bottles, containers, and packaging in the designated recycling bin. Make sure to rinse them before recycling.

Items like plastic bags and wraps can be taken to local grocery store recycling bins.

Paper

Recycle newspapers, magazines, office paper, and cardboard. Flatten cardboard boxes before recycling.

Avoid recycling paper products contaminated with food or liquids.

Glass

Recycle glass bottles and jars. Rinse them thoroughly to remove any residues.

Do not include broken glass, drinking glasses, or ceramics in the glass recycling bin.

Metal

Recycle aluminum cans, steel cans, and clean aluminum foil. Rinse them to remove any remaining substances.

Avoid putting metal items with food residue in the recycling bin.
